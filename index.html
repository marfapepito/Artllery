THE SHAPE OF MEMORY MIDNIGHT SOCIETY
Dennis 'Sio' Montera Yxandro
9 OCT - 5 DEC 2021 13 NOV - 10 DEC 2021
It brings to the foreground the texture, the pigments, the colors, It is as if a neo-impressionist from the 1800s existed in the present
and all other visual elements in order to let them speak for themselves. and instead of the bucolic he paints the vigor and joy of his fleeting youth
Form no longer hides beneath familiar imagery and narrative. in the city in hazy imagery dazed by the flickering in bars and light posts.
